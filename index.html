Welcome to The Clash of the Trolls!

The forest is a challenging place to find food and water. Only the strongest troll always survives. Reach the other side of the forest before your oponent. The rules are simple: there are no rules! Finish as fast as you can.
-Player 1 plays by pressing "Right Arrow" key. -Player 2 plays by pressing "Z" key.
